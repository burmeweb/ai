Our Story

Wayne AI was founded with a simple mission: to make artificial intelligence accessible and useful for everyone. Our journey began in 2023 when our founder, Aung Myo Kyaw, recognized the need for an AI assistant that could understand context, remember conversations, and provide truly helpful responses.

Today, Wayne AI serves thousands of users worldwide, helping them with everything from creative writing to coding, from research to everyday tasks. We're proud of what we've built, but we're even more excited about where we're going.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To democratize artificial intelligence by creating intuitive, powerful tools that enhance human creativity and productivity.
